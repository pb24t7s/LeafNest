Growing your own microgreens and sprouts is easy, rewarding, and takes just minutes a day!
Follow our simple step-by-step guide below.

Prepare Growing Medium

Soak cocopeat block in water for 15-20 minutes until fully expanded. Spread evenly in your growing tray, about 1-2 inches deep.

Need: Cocopeat, Tray
2
ðŸŒ±

Sow Seeds

Spread seeds evenly across the moist cocopeat. Lightly press them down. Mist with water using spray bottle.

Need: Seeds, Spray Bottle
3
ðŸ 

Cover & Germinate

Cover with humidity dome or another tray. Keep in dark place for 2-3 days. Spray water twice daily to keep moist.

Need: Humidity Dome
4
â˜€ï¸

Provide Light

After germination, move to bright indirect sunlight or under grow lights. Continue misting 2-3 times daily.

Need: Grow Light (optional)
5
ðŸŒ¿

Grow & Monitor

Watch your microgreens grow! Keep soil moist but not waterlogged. Ensure good air circulation to prevent mold.

Time: 5-12 days
6
âœ‚ï¸

Harvest & Enjoy

When first true leaves appear, use scissors to cut just above soil level. Rinse, pat dry, and enjoy fresh!

Need: Harvesting Scissors

Frequency: Every 8-12 hours

Watch Them Grow

Sprouts will grow quickly! After 3-5 days, you'll see beautiful, crunchy sprouts ready to eat. Continue rinsing daily.

Time: 3-5 days

Final Rinse & Enjoy

Give one final thorough rinse. Drain well and store in refrigerator. Use within 3-5 days for best freshness!
